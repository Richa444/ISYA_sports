About US

About Us:The India Young Sporting Alliance is registered as a Non Profitable Charitable Trust under the Society Registration Act of 1882 by Murugaraj. For youth who are living on the streets and in various underdeveloped communities deprived of education and sports training due to poverty and prone to bad habits, we provide services to improve sports. Our mission is to encourage children to focus on sports and education so that their lives can be holistically developed, as well as those of their families. Through sports, we can encourage young people to discover, enjoy, and stimulate
... themselves people by giving them the opportunity to enjoy, stimulate and discover through sports.
