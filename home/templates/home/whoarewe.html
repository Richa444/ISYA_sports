Who we are

----action speaks larger----

India Young Sporting Alliance is a registered Non-Profit Charitable Trust, We provide services to improve sports for youth, especially on the streets and in various underdeveloped communities, who are deprived of education and sports training due to poverty and are prone to bad habits, through sports coaches and counsellors to encourage children to focus on sports and education and the holistic development of their own lives and the lives of their families as a whole. We promote the value of young people by giving them the opportunity to enjoy, stimulate and discover through sports.
