The Karnataka State Football Association and the Bangalore District Football Association (BDFA) organised a C Division Football... League on November 14, 2022 at the KSFA grounds in Ashok Nagar in which we competed against 6 teams, won 5 matches and drew against one team.

Youth Sports Day on January 25, 2023.

"IYSA (India Young Sporting Alliance) hosted the Youth Sports Day at Sumanahalli on January 25, 2023 Director of IYSA,... Murugaraj, emphasized the importance of physical activities for a healthy mind. Mr. Kamal, BUFC Super Division Football Coach, Bengaluru, encouraged participation over winning, pledging ongoing support to IYSA. The event featured diverse sports activities tailored for sub-juniors, juniors, and seniors, with medals and certificates awarded to winners by the directors and staff. Mr. Harsha, physical teacher at IYSA Bengaluru, extended gratitude, as over 70 children from IYSA's centers and intervention areas actively participated."

Sports Day on February 6,2023.

A vibrant Sports Day was orchestrated for the youth of IYSA at the National Ground in Basavanagudi on February 6, ... 2023. The event served as a platform for participants to showcase their talents, unwind, and relish the day. Under the guidance of IYSA Director, Mr. Murugaraj, boys and girls engaged in various events including Football, Tug of War, Cricket, Throwball, and Kabaddi. A total of 42 boys and 35 girls enthusiastically participated, fostering a spirit of sportsmanship and camaraderie.

Health Awareness on December 10, 2022

"IYSA, Chamarajpet, collaborated with BOSCO Health Care Department to host an awareness session on December... 10, 2022, tackling the harmful effects of tobacco and drug use. Director Mr. Murugaraj welcomed the attendees, while Fr. Jude Anand from BOSCO Nilaya delivered an enlightening talk on substance abuse. Through a PowerPoint presentation, participants gained insights into the issue, followed by a skit performed by MSW interns illustrating strategies to overcome addiction and embrace a brighter future in sports."
